About Me

I'm a freelance Web Designer based in New Delhi, India.

I specialise in responsive front-end development & web typography and build simple, yet elegant websites with the best flavours of HTML5 and CSS3. I am passionate about good design, clean code and the intersection of the two.

Currently, I am Creative Director at MilkyWay, a free android game developed by Hasan Kamal.

I am sometimes also engaged in Branding Design with Aman Srivastava.

While not working, I can be found tapping my feet to House Music, watching Manchester United matches and dropping a jaw or two seeing good design.

This site is completely responsive and fluid and will adapt to the device used to view it with. The typefaces used are Quattrocento Sans by Pablo Impallari & Lusitana by Ana Paula Megda. All typefaces are served through the magic of Google Web Fonts.
